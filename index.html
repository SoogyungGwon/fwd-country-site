Dokdo

About

Dokdo is an island located in the easternmost part of Korea. Administratively, it belongs to Eulleung-eup, Ulleung-gun, Gyeongsangbuk-do, and its coordinates are 37 degrees 14 minutes 26.8 seconds north latitude and 131 degrees 52 minutes 10.4 seconds east longitude. Looking at the distance from major points, it is located 87.4km southeast of Ulleungdo Island, and 157.5km northwest of Japan's Oki Island.Ulleungdo and Dokdo are visible to each other when the weather is clear, but Dokdo is not visible from Oki Island.
